How to get HWID

In order to get your hwid download hwid finder which you can get here.

Download HWID-Finder.rar after that extract the rar file, open HWID-Finder.bat, then your hwid will be sent in the console.

Reminder: In order to use HWID-Finder you must have nodejs installed.
